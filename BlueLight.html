EFFECTS

Blue light is light that comes from screens (computer screens, phone screens, TVs,...).It has many effect on humans as well also on animals. There are many studies that show us how bad it is for people, especially for children and babies.

SLEEP

There are many studies on how blue light effects our sleep, but not all are scientifficly proven. We must not believe everything that we read. But you will for sure sleep better if you will shout down all screens two hours before you go to sleep.
